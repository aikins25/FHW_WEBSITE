Our History

From Humble Beginnings

Founded with a vision to bridge the digital skills gap among Ghanaian youth

Empowering Youth Since 2022

Fit Home Well was officially launched on October 31, 2022, by a group of tech professionals and educators who saw the urgent need for practical digital skills training among Ghana's youth.

What began as a small initiative with 20 students in a community center has grown into a recognized NGO that has impacted hundreds of young lives across multiple regions.

2022

Foundation

FHW launched with its first cohort of 20 students in Accra, focusing on basic computer literacy and graphic design.

2023

Expansion

Added Python programming and crypto trading courses in response to market demand, serving 150 students annually.

2025

2025 Edition

Launched our most comprehensive program yet, with advanced modules and partnerships with tech companies.
